Introduction

This project will analyze the 311 graffiti and street light outage service requests in Chicago, listed by neighborhood, and crime data of Chicago. By measuring the crime statistics versus the service requests, we can find out whether there is a link between crime and areas tagged by graffiti, or crime and lowly lit areas. If such a link exists, then we can help the public by suggesting to increase the effort in removing graffiti to have cleaner neighborhoods, and to add/repair street lights to have more visibility at night.
